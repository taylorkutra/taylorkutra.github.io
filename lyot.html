Opacity-wavelength Dependance

Simulations with lower opacity are unstable to longer wavelength perturbations because the wave must have a larger radial width for low opacities to be opaque. We note that the degree to which the meridional flows evacuate material out of the temperature wave is also a funciton of opacity -- larger opacities create deeper, thinner, gaps. This video shows a long thermal time simulation (tth > tdyn ) where κ is 10 times greater than those above.
