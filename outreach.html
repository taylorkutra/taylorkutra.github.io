Outreach

Science communication and public outreach is a priority of mine. My main goal with my outreach is to make astronomy more approachable by encouraging and promoting accessible communication. I am involved in several regular outreach events which are described briefly below.

Also check out my Cosmos From Your Couch episodes, and my AstroTours talk for International Womens Day.

AstroTours

This is a monthly event put on by graduate students in the Department of Astronomy and Astrophysics. There is typically a one hour talk by a student or postdoc followed by exhibits and planetarium shows.

  • Learn More

Astronomy on Tap

This popular event is put on by the Dunlap Institute and typically happens every 3 months. It is an evening of entertaining talks with other games and plenty of roaming astronomers.
