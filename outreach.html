Outreach

My primary goal with my outreach is to make astronomy more inclusive and accesible for people who have been systematically marginalized or historically excluded. I focus not only on effectively communicating complex technical ideas, but also on centering stories of astronomy in which audiences can see themselves reflected and to which they can feel connected.

Canyon Explorations

I am an Astronomer in Grand Canyon with Canyon Explorations/Expeditions. These trips combine the magnificent nature of Grand Canyon with dark skies. Over the course of 16 days, we look at everything from the Moon to star-forming nebula and galaxies.

Astronomy on Tap
